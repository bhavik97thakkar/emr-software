## 🚀 Advanced Clinical Features (New & Updated)

### 1. AI-Powered Voice Input & Refinement
*   **Speech-to-Text Clinical Entry:** Record symptoms, diagnosis, and advice simply by speaking. No more tedious typing.
*   **Smart Note Refinement:** One-click AI refinement to polish your spoken notes into professional medical terminology.

### 2. Intelligent Consultation Flow
*   **Current Medication Tracking (Ongoing Meds):** Dedicated tracking for medicines prescribed by other doctors. Prevents drug-drug interaction confusion.
*   **Comprehensive Vitals & History:** Instantly record Weight, BP, SpO2, and Temp with BMI auto-calculation.
*   **Chronic Record Vault:** Attach past prescriptions, X-rays, and Lab reports (PDF/Images) directly to the patient's digital profile.

### 3. Smart Automation & Printing
*   **Automatic Background Cloud Sync:** Every entry you save is automatically backed up to the cloud within 3 seconds of inactivity.
*   **Offline-First Resilience:** The app works 100% offline. If the internet fails, you keep working; it syncs quietly when the connection returns.
*   **Digital Prescription (Rx) Designer:** Generate premium, branded PDF prescriptions with one click. Share instantly via **WhatsApp** or **Email**.

### 4. Practice Intelligence
*   **Automated Clinical Ledger:** Track daily revenue, settlement modes (Cash, UPI, GPay), and outstanding dues automatically.
*   **Disease Analytics:** Track the frequency of diagnoses to understand your most common patient profiles.
